Lasagna

Description

A classic recipe of one of the most loved foods in the world. The ultimate comfort food is the humble homemade lasagna. Family and friends gather around the dinner table at any time for this lasagna recipe. A big warm comforting hug within layers of pasta, tomato sauce, white sauce and melted cheese!

This recipe is not exactly ideal to look at and salivate over in a month that we are all on our low carb, Keto, paleo, weight watchers, cabbage soup, low fat, low carb, rabbit food diets. But...It's Lasagna.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Ground Beef
  • Passata
  • Crushed Tomatoes
  • Parmesan
